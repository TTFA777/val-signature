Our Services

What We
Provide

Val Signature responds to the rapidly evolving transport sector by providing structured driver recruitment, training, and placement solutions for individual fleet owners and SMEs in the transport industry.

01

Driver Recruitment & Vetting

Rigorous screening and vetting: licence verification, background and criminal checks, identity verification, customer service evaluation, and professional driver suitability assessment. Clients receive trusted, competent drivers.

02

Professional Driver Training

Structured training including platform onboarding (e-hailing), driver etiquette and ratings management, trip efficiency and navigation, customer interaction best practices, and income optimisation strategies.

03

Fleet Owner Training

Employment contract drafting, fleet management tools, people management, moving from self-employment to business management in the e-hailing industry, cost containment, and compliance.

04

Corporate Driver Placement

Placing trained drivers in corporate transport, logistics, shuttle services, e-hailing fleets, and electric vehicle operations. Trained, compliant and dependable drivers for every need.
